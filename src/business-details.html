About Us:

Welcome to A Touch of Salt on Ogden Street in the heart of Townsville's CBD. We are a family owned, multi-award winning restaurant featuring an ever-evolving fine-dining menu, extensive wine cellar and an uncompromised approach to service. Immaculately presented, bursting with flavour and available in gluten-free alternatives, our exciting restaurant menus are a feast for the senses. We also offer gift vouchers. For more details visit our website: www.atouchofsalt.com.au

Weddings:
